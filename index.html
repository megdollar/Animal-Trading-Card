Lorikeet

The Lorikeet is a remarkable bird with plumage in contrasting colors and shades.

  • Scientific Name: Trichoglossus haematodus moluccanus
  • Average Length: 30 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 25-28 years
  • Habitat: Eastern Australia and Southern Australia

The Lorikeet's feathers consist of many different colors from blue to red to green. There are over twenty sub-species of the Lorikeet. They are very noisy birds with a natural ability to mimic. The Lorikeet requires a lot of exercise and should never have clipped wings.
